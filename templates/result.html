How to enhance the image?

Follow the bellow instructions, which will help you to enhance your image .

Access the X-ray Image Enhancer websites:

Visit the X-ray Image Enhancer platform or application through your web browser or designated access point.

Upload Image:

Locate the option to upload an image. This may be labeled as "Upload Image" or similar. Click on this option to select the X-ray image file you wish to enhance from your device's storage.

Processing:

After selecting the image file, wait for the X-ray Image Enhancer to process the uploaded image. Depending on the size and complexity of the image, processing times may vary. The system will automatically apply enhancement algorithms to improve the quality of the image.

Download Enhanced Image:

If satisfied with the enhancement, you may have the option to download the enhanced image to your device for further analysis or documentation. Look for a "Download" button or similar option to initiate the download process.
